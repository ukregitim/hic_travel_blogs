Top Picks For Authentic Turkish Cuisine

For those seeking the true flavors of Anatolia, these establishments are local institutions.

Ravza Restaurant: One of the oldest and most beloved spots in the city center. It is famous for its Iskender Kebab and traditional Turkish appetizers (mezes). The atmosphere is warm, family-oriented, and authentic.

Mezze Grill Ocakba≈üƒ±: Located near the harbor, this is the go-to for a "barbecue" experience. They specialize in lamb shish and Adana kebabs cooked over an open charcoal grill.

Esma Hanƒ±m‚Äôƒ±n Yeri: If you want "home-cooked" style food, this is the place. It features Alanya-specific dishes like Sulu Yemek (stews) that you won't easily find in tourist-heavy bistros.

Best Dining With A View (Romantic & Fine Dining)

Alanya‚Äôs geography allows for some of the most dramatic restaurant settings in Turkey.

Kale Panorama Restaurant: Perched high on the way to the Castle, it offers a 270-degree view of the harbor and Cleopatra Beach. It is perfect for a sunset dinner; they even offer a free shuttle service from many hotels.

The Rabbit: Famous for its "I LOVE ALANYA" sign, this restaurant provides a modern, chic atmosphere with a panoramic view from the Taurus Mountains.

Gardenia Restaurant: Situated on the castle hill, it blends European fine dining with stunning vistas. It is widely considered one of the most romantic spots in the city.

Mahperi Restaurant: A historic waterfront choice since 1947. Located right in the harbor, it‚Äôs known for its professional service and a mix of fresh seafood and international steaks.

Fresh Seafood & Fish Houses

In Alanya, the rule is simple: if you want the best fish, head to the harbor or the dedicated "Fish Market."

Alanya Balƒ±k Evi (Alanya Fish House): Known for its elegant presentation and "catch of the day" selection. Their Sea Bass (Levrek) and Calamari are consistently highly rated.

Kalei√ßi Meyhanesi: A classic Turkish Meyhane (tavern) experience. Expect fresh fish, flowing Raki, and a vibrant atmosphere where locals gather for long, social dinners.

Saray Balƒ±k: Located near the central fish market, this is a more casual, high-turnover spot where the focus is purely on the freshness of the seafood.

International & Casual Gems

If you need a break from Turkish spices, Alanya has excellent global options.

Bistro Bahama: A lively, popular spot near Cleopatra Beach serving a huge menu ranging from Mexican fajitas to Italian pastas. Great for families.

Enberi Cafe: Known for having some of the best Italian-style pizzas and a modern, trendy vibe.

Soul of Kitchen: A stylish Mediterranean fusion restaurant that focuses on high-quality ingredients and modern culinary techniques.
