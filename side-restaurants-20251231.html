Fine Dining: Upscale & Elegant

For those "dress-up" nights where the service is as polished as the silverware.

Elia Restaurant & Lounge: Arguably the most sophisticated spot in the Old Town. It blends ancient stone architecture with modern sustainable luxury. The wine list is extensive, featuring top-tier Turkish labels from the Aegean region.

Karma Restaurant & Night Club: While it turns into a high-end club late at night, the dinner service is impeccable. It offers a cosmopolitan vibe with a menu that bridges the gap between East and West.

Local Favorites: Authentic & Affordable

Best For Views: Dinner With A Backdrop

In Side, the view usually involves two things: the sparkling Mediterranean or the pillars of a 2,000-year-old temple.

Apollonik Cafe: Located literally steps from the Temple of Apollo. There is no better place in the world to sip a cocktail and watch the sun dip below the horizon through ancient columns.

Zula Side: Perched with an elevated view of the harbor, Zula offers a panoramic vista of the boats and the sea. The blue-and-white decor gives it a distinct "Greek Isles" feel.
