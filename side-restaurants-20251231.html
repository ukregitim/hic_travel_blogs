ELIA Restaurant & Lounge

Situated right by the harbor, Elia offers a sophisticated "chill-out" vibe with a breathtaking sunset view. It is widely considered the premier fine-dining destination for those who appreciate high-end Mediterranean flavors and an extensive wine list.

The Experience: Romantic, candle-lit harbor views with a focus on sustainable, local ingredients.

Highlight: Grilled Octopus and the Lamb Shank, paired with a selection from their curated Turkish wine cellar.

Liman Restaurant Lounge Club

Located in the most romantic part of the Old Town, Liman is a Side institution. It successfully bridges the gap between a high-end restaurant and a lounge club, making it perfect for a long, celebratory evening.

The Experience: Upscale seaside dining with a "white-tablecloth" feel during sunset, transitioning into a vibrant lounge atmosphere.

Highlight: The Seafood Platter for two and their signature sunset cocktails.
